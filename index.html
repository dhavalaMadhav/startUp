About NeonBoard

NeonBoard is pioneering the future of outdoor advertising in India, starting with our flagship digital billboard in the heart of Visakhapatnam. Our vision is to transform static cityscapes into dynamic, engaging visual experiences that benefit both advertisers and the community.

Founded in 2023, we're bringing cutting-edge LED technology to Vizag's advertising landscape, offering unprecedented flexibility, targeting capabilities, and environmental benefits compared to traditional hoardings.

Our first installation at Rushikonda Beach Road has already become a landmark, showcasing how digital billboards can enhance urban environments while delivering superior advertising performance.
