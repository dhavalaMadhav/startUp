Data Analytics Process

Our comprehensive 6-phase analytics system transforms billboard advertising into data-driven marketing

Phase 1: Data Collection
AI-powered cameras, motion sensors, and mobile location tracking capture real-time audience data. We collect impressions, demographics (age, gender), traffic patterns, and environmental conditions including weather and time-of-day metrics.
Phase 2: Data Processing
Raw data is cleaned, validated, and structured using machine learning algorithms. Mobile Advertising IDs (MAIDs) are matched with location data, achieving 63% match rates for Android and 58% for iOS devices within 500-800 feet of billboards.
Phase 4: Real-Time Dashboard
Live performance monitoring displays impression counts, audience demographics, engagement rates, and cost-per-thousand (CPM) metrics. Advertisers access comprehensive reports showing campaign effectiveness across all billboard locations simultaneously.
Phase 3: Analysis & Insights
Advanced analytics engine calculates key metrics: dwell time, engagement rates, conversion tracking, and impression multipliers. We analyze QR code scans, website visits, and app activity to measure direct response and attribution.
Phase 5: Optimization
AI-driven content scheduling adjusts ad displays based on audience patterns and performance data. Dynamic optimization considers peak traffic hours, weather conditions, and real-time engagement to maximize campaign impact and ROI.
Phase 6: Results & Attribution
Comprehensive reporting measures brand lift (14% increase over 180 days), store visit attribution (28% increase within 90 days), branded search growth (17% over 120 days), and overall conversion rates. Quarterly brand lift studies validate long-term campaign effectiveness.
